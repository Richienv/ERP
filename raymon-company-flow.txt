## 1. Gambaran Umum Alur Bisnis RISE

- Bisnis: manufaktur tekstil / bedding / fashion (ada gudang kain, aksesoris, produksi, showroom, online). [uphance](https://www.uphance.com/blog/garment-manufacturing/)
- Alur besar:  
  1) R&D + Desainer buat desain & BOM →  
  2) Purchasing & Gudang bahan baku siapkan kain & aksesoris →  
  3) Produksi & sewing jalan →  
  4) Barang masuk Gudang Jadi →  
  5) Distribusi ke showroom/toko/online via Admin Pengiriman →  
  6) Penjualan dicatat Finance & tim penjualan →  
  7) Stok diawasi Inventory Control. [onlineclothingstudy](https://www.onlineclothingstudy.com/2013/05/garment-manufacturing-process-flow-chart.html)

## 2. Flow Proses Utama (Ringkas tapi Nyambung)

### A. Desain & Perencanaan Produksi

Peran utama: **R&D** dan **Desainer**.

- R&D:
  - Membuat pola & desain model (JS Sample) sesuai order kerja. [techpacker](https://techpacker.com/blog/manufacturing/12-types-of-garment-samples-you-should-know-about-for-apparel-production/)
  - Mencoba produk baru, menganalisa hasil, membuat BOM (Bill of Material), Kartu Produksi (KP), dan rencana produksi. [textilelearner](https://textilelearner.net/flow-chart-of-garment-sampling-process/)
  - Koordinasi dengan sample, cutting, gudang kain, gudang aksesoris, dan sewing. [shanghaigarment](https://shanghaigarment.com/garment-production-process-2/)
- Desainer:
  - Bantu R&D dalam desain produk baru, pilih motif, warna, bahan, bersama marketing & direktur. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)
  - Buat dokumen bahan kain baru untuk gudang kain, PPIC, produksi; cek kesesuaian warna, ukuran, bahan, hasil jadi. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)

**Data yang dibutuhkan tahap ini:**

- Desain/model, pola, ukuran.  
- BOM (kain, aksesoris, label, packing, dll).  
- Rencana produksi per bulan & target SKU. [uphance](https://www.uphance.com/blog/garment-manufacturing/)

***

### B. Pengadaan & Gudang Bahan

Peran utama: **Gudang Bahan Baku & Aksesoris**, **Gudang Kain**, **Pembelian (AP)**.

- Gudang Bahan Baku & Aksesoris:
  - Terima, simpan, dan keluarkan bahan baku/aksesoris sesuai Job Order (JO) & Pick List. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)
  - Buat LPB (Laporan Penerimaan Barang), bukti keluar barang, laporan mutasi stock, ajukan PR/PP kalau stok kurang. [onlineclothingstudy](https://www.onlineclothingstudy.com/2023/05/production-progress-report-and-format.html)
  - Jaga kesesuaian stok fisik vs data (stock opname berkala). [onlineclothingstudy](https://www.onlineclothingstudy.com/2018/05/how-to-make-wip-report-in-garment.html)
- Gudang Kain:
  - Terima kain dari supplier, cek dengan surat jalan, buat PO & LPB, dan catat pemakaian kain yang sudah di‑cut. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)
  - Keluarkan kain sesuai JO/kepala produksi dan pastikan stok komputer = stok fisik. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)

**Data yang dibutuhkan:**

- Dokumen SJ supplier, LPB, PO, Job Order, Pick List. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)
- Stok per item (kain, aksesoris, handuk, plastik, layer, dacron) dan mutasi per bulan. [onlineclothingstudy](https://www.onlineclothingstudy.com/2018/05/how-to-make-wip-report-in-garment.html)

### C. Produksi & Barang Jadi

Peran utama: **Produksi / Sewing**, **Gudang Barang Jadi**, **Admin Pengiriman**.

- Produksi:
  - Menerima bahan dari gudang via JO, proses cutting & sewing menurut pola dan BOM. [shanghaigarment](https://shanghaigarment.com/garment-production-process-2/)
- Gudang Barang Jadi:
  - Terima barang jadi dari produksi/makloon, simpan sebagai stok barang jadi. [onlineclothingstudy](https://www.onlineclothingstudy.com/2013/05/garment-manufacturing-process-flow-chart.html)
- Admin Pengiriman:
  - Menjamin pengiriman tepat waktu dan sesuai jenis/qty. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)
  - Atur personel distribusi, kendaraan, dan penyusunan barang di mobil. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)
  - Buat dan kontrol surat jalan untuk segala jenis pergerakan: sample, makloon, online, retur, ekspedisi, dll. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)

**Data yang dibutuhkan:**

- Kartu stok barang jadi per artikel.  
- Surat jalan makloon/sablon/bordir, packing list makloon. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)
- Jadwal pengiriman & prioritas DO (delivery order). [onlineclothingstudy](https://www.onlineclothingstudy.com/2023/05/production-progress-report-and-format.html)

### D. Distribusi ke Toko / Online & Penjualan

Peran utama: **Marketing Showroom**, **Tim Sales/Koordinator**, **Finance**, **Admin Pengiriman**.

- Marketing Showroom:
  - Rencanakan dan awasi penjualan showroom, buat sales forecast, alokasi barang ke toko, analisa trend & slow moving. [uphance](https://www.uphance.com/blog/garment-manufacturing/)
  - Handle klaim customer showroom, retur dari counter, laporan stok bulanan showroom. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)
- Tim pemasaran (di bagian lain file):
  - Cari area bazar/pop‑up, kontrol SPG/SPB, pantau omzet harian/mingguan/bulanan, koordinasi dengan dept store. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)
- Finance:
  - Kelola penerimaan & pengeluaran kas/bank, cashflow, anggaran, serta laporan keuangan. [pearlglobal](https://www.pearlglobal.com/garments-manufacturing-process/)
  - Rekap penjualan bazar, laporan penjualan toko online, cek barang online sebelum kirim. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)
- Admin Pengiriman:
  - Untuk barang online: setelah packing → konfirmasi ke purchasing untuk minta driver kirim. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)
  - Follow up ekspedisi & tagihan ekspedisi (Indah Logistic, Wibowo, dsb). [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)

**Data yang dibutuhkan:**

- Sales forecast per periode & realisasi per channel (showroom, dept store, bazar, online). [onlineclothingstudy](https://www.onlineclothingstudy.com/2023/05/production-progress-report-and-format.html)
- DO, surat jalan, resi ekspedisi, tagihan ekspedisi, berita acara kalau ada kerusakan. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)
- Laporan penjualan online, bazar, showroom. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)

### E. Kontrol Stok & Rekonsiliasi

Peran utama: **Inventory Control**, **Admin Stok/Finance**.

- Inventory Control:
  - Stock opname berkala untuk barang jadi di gudang & counter. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)
  - Proses, audit, dan koreksi hasil stok opname, buat rincian biaya kehilangan kalau ada selisih. [textilelearner](https://textilelearner.net/how-to-calculate-work-in-progress-wip-in-garment-industry/)
  - Transfer order antar gudang, koreksi artikel/SKU, rekap surat jalan Direksi & brand khusus (Cacoon), buat invoice dan catatan penjualan brand tersebut. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)
- Admin yang terkait stok & penjualan:
  - Dampingi stok opname kas kecil, buat laporan detail penjualan per counter, rekonsiliasi penjualan vs tagihan accounting, dan rincian penerimaan piutang. [ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/80524430/bdc69238-4de4-44a0-86ea-23d4a00c3561/JOBDESK-STAFF-RISE.pdf)

**Data yang dibutuhkan:**

- Hasil stok opname (fisik vs komputer) per gudang/counter. [onlineclothingstudy](https://www.onlineclothingstudy.com/2018/05/how-to-make-wip-report-in-garment.html)
- Rekap surat jalan, mutasi stok, laporan kehilangan/selisih.  
- Rekonsiliasi penjualan, piutang, dan kas kecil. [onlineclothingstudy](https://www.onlineclothingstudy.com/2023/05/production-progress-report-and-format.html)
